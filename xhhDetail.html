Recharge

Users can choose their preferred banks to recharge funds into their own platform accounts,upon success, a new financial record will be generated.

Investment

From the investment list, investors can choose projects they are interested in.

Investors can review project detail, and proceed to make investments.

Investment confirm

If users have coupons, they can use them for investments. They need to enter the investment amount and payment password. After submission, if the investment criteria are met, they will be directed to the investment success page.

Message centre

Investors will receive message notifications in the message center for each important milestone. For instance, during registration, fund recharge, investment, returns, withdrawal, and other relevant steps.

Sign Electronic Contracts

After the project reaches its funding goal, the platform will electronically sign contracts for each investor.

Repayment page

After the borrower's loan matures, the platform needs to distribute investment returns to each project. This process is referred to as the repayment operation.

Both the account homepage and the repayment calendar will display repayment reminders, and a new repayment entry will be added to the fund records.

Withdrawl

After investors acquired investment returns, they can withdraw their earnings to their bank accounts.
