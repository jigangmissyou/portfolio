Sign In

Users can log in with their Weltec and Whitireia accounts, and I'm using Microsoft Entra ID to access internal resources at Weltec and Whitireia. This is similar to the SSO (Single Sign-On) technology, which means you only need one set of login credentials to access multiple services.

Booking

In the bookings page, users can choose the dates and routes they want to ride.

Once users confirm their bookings, they'll get a confirmation email. They can also cancel their bookings on the booking management page if needed.

Settings

Administrator can set configurations of the applicaton in the bookings setting page. Such as set unavailable booking dates, the number of days in advance during which students are allowed to make bookings for services, etc.

Verification

Users need to scan the QR code to verify their shuttle bus booking to make sure they don't miss their scheduled departure time. If they do miss the bus, their "missed bus" count will go up. If this count exceeds the limit set by the administrator, they won't be allowed to make future bookings.

Students management

A student management page keeps track of students' information and records their bookings for a shuttle bus. The administrator can help students if they've missed the bus too many times by allowing them to use the shuttle again.
