Detail page

Clicking on 'Details' allows you to view the specifics of each booking and shipping order.

Audit Process

Each booking order is accompanied by a notification when it transitions to the responsible personnel, prompting them to conduct the review. Additionally, you can monitor the current stage of the review process.

Booking Modification

Order changes, booking modifications, and shipping updates are all possible scenarios. The system should meticulously record the details of each change and notify the responsible personnel of the subsequent steps in the change process.

Process Configuration

All processes, including audit processes and change processes, can be customized through backend configuration.This allows for flexible and convenient management of workflows.

Exporting Documents

Supports batch exporting of various customs-compliant documents in formats such as PDF and Excel.
