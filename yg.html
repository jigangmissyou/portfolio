Foreign Trade Import-Export Platform

Main features

Clearly display which booking orders have been shipped and which ones are pending shipment, facilitating tracking for sales and follow-up personnel to monitor each booking order.
View more features ...

Technologies and Tools Used

Throughout the project, I utilized various technologies and tools, including but not limited to:
  • Front-end: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, LayUi
  • Back-end: PHP, ThinkPHP framework,MySQL,C#,SqlServer,Redis
  • Version Control: Git

Project Goals

  • Create a centralized system that streamlines and optimizes various stages of the import and export process.
  • Enhance operational efficiency by providing real-time visibility into the status of each booking order, ensuring timely deliveries and reducing delays.
  • Facilitate effective communication and collaboration between sales personnel and follow-up teams by providing a unified platform for tracking and managing orders.

Achievements

  • Free theirs hands from manually calculate and manage data related to orders, booking records, and other logistical information.
  • Greately reduced the probability of human errors in complex workflows.
  • Dramatically improved the efficiency of sales personnel and customs document officers(According to their year-end report, their work efficiency has improved by 300%).

Project Challenges and Solutions

  • High levels of customization and complex workflows resulting in intricate features and potential management complexities.
  • To address this, I work closely with sales and customs documentation teams, and learning business operations from them which was essential to ensure accurate alignment of the system with their specific requirements.
  • Customized approval workflows and notifications, making the approval processes smoother and more efficient.

Self-Assessment

The manufacturing industry is a domain unfamiliar to me. The production processes within this field are highly complex and frequently subject to changes. The challenge of our system lies in maintaining stability amidst constant variations. I've dedicated considerable effort to researching and studying critical aspects such as authorization control, process management, and addressing evolving business requirements. I've formulated a comprehensive and tailored solution to address various challenges in production and sales. Moreover, I've strived to make each process segment configurable in the backend, ensuring ease of future process adaptations.
