Foreign Trade Import-Export Platform

Project Overview

Developed a comprehensive export system catering specifically to manufacturing industries in the foreign trade import-export sector. The system aimed to streamline and automate complex processes, including booking management, container loading, shipment handling, order modifications, workflow approval, invoice generation, and customs document printing.

Technologies and Tools Used

Throughout the project, I utilized various technologies and tools, including but not limited to:
  • Front-end: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, LayUi
  • Back-end: PHP, ThinkPHP framework,MySQL,C#,SqlServer,Redis
  • Version Control: Git

Project Goals and Achievements

  • Dramatically improved the efficiency of sales personnel and customs document officers.
  • Reduced the probability of human errors in complex export workflows.
  • Empowered manufacturing industries with a specialized solution tailored to their unique needs.

Project Challenges and Solutions

  • High levels of customization and complex workflows resulting in intricate features and potential management complexities.
  • To address this, I work closely with sales and customs documentation teams, and learning business operations from them which was essential to ensure accurate alignment of the system with their specific requirements
  • I created customized approval workflows and notifications in the backend for different departments, making the approval processes smoother and more efficient.

Self-Assessment

This experience pushed me out of my comfort zone and fostered personal growth. It built my confidence in tackling challenging tasks and expanding my skill set to cater to the unique demands of the manufacturing sector.
