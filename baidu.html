Introduction

During my tenure as a Senior PHP Developer at Baidu's Map department, I had the opportunity to maintain and develop the Public Transportation Platform, playing a crucial role in its continuous improvement and evolution. Baidu, a global AI and Internet company, tasked me with improving the management of the nation's public transport systems, including buses and subways. By introducing a new algorithm, I successfully boosted task assignment accuracy for map data collectors by an impressive 50%. Furthermore, I streamlined the data upload process in Baidu Map Transit Tycoon app, leading to a remarkable 40% increase in the success rate for handling large collection packages. These enhancements played a pivotal role in reducing user complaints by 30%.
